# ShellOrchestra Edition Limits

This file is a human-readable summary. The binding terms are in `LICENSE.md` and any separate commercial agreement signed with Develastic, s. r. o.

## Community Edition

Community Edition is free for Internal Use, including business, commercial, and production environments.

Current Community Edition limits:

- up to 10 server profiles;
- Pro-only features are disabled;
- license checks, feature gates, server-profile limits, and Pro-only restrictions must not be bypassed.

Community Edition is intended to let individuals, homelab users, administrators, and businesses evaluate and use ShellOrchestra safely without an upfront commercial barrier.

## Pro Edition

Pro Edition is a commercial edition. Planned Pro rights include:

- no Community server-profile limit;
- access to Pro features;
- commercial update/support terms according to the paid plan.

Pro Edition is still for the customer's own Internal Use unless a separate written agreement says otherwise.

## Rights not included in standard editions

Community and Pro licenses do not automatically grant redistribution, resale, hosted-service, MSP, OEM, white-label, or public-fork rights. Those rights require a separate written agreement with Develastic, s. r. o.

## Enforcement Philosophy

ShellOrchestra may use light or nominal technical protection for Pro features. The license still applies. If a limit can technically be bypassed, that does not mean bypassing it is permitted.
